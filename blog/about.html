The story behind Mendax

Mendax is a biological and medical science laboratory founded by Enzo Contrucci in 2018. We specialize in small, simple and seemingly useless experiments to gain more knowledge about organisms. Our slogan "where science lives" is a metaphor that tells the story of our beginning as we started out in the bedroom of Contrucci back in 2018. When Enzo got a microscope he wanted to test random biological things, thus our research began. Now consisting of three scientists Mendax BioSciences Lab is ever expanding and growing. The word mendax comes from the Latin translation of a liar as we are inexperienced and do not know what we are talking about every time but post any information we our selves see and acknowledge.
